About The Coney

Proudly Serving Schuylkill County Since 1917

The original Coney Island Pottsville was established on Centre Street in the Downtown district of Pottsville, Pennsylvania. In 1917, downtown Pottsville served as the social center and gateway to Schuylkill County's bountiful anthracite coal region.

Today's location, nestled in the heart of Pottsville's Yorkville district (only a couple miles up the road), serves our community 24/7 with the same family recipe and a twist of contemporary fun. We owe all of our success, past and future, to our hard working staff and loyal patrons.
